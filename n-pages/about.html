About Us

Our organization is dedicated to the vision of conserving, protecting, and sustainably utilizing the oceans, seas, and marine resources for the betterment of our planet.

GOALS

The primary goals that we as an organisation are trying to achieve when it comes to the resources for further sustainable development are to able to promote the responsible and sustainable ways fishing practices, such as reducing over fishing. To reduce pollution and of the countless waste in our oceans and seas, such as plastic waste and To advertise the policies and regulations that protect and preserve marine life and ecosystems that live in the waters.

VISION

We as an organisation hope in the near future we can achieve our goals and wants into our oceans. In the future we see the proper and sustainable impliment of fishing practices, instilled a community that helps preserve and sustain the current marine ecosystem and we hope to have taken alarge quantity of the oceans countless waste out of our oceans

ACHIEVEMENTS

At this current moment there has been some progress in expanding the marine protected areas, along with stopping unregulated fishing and banning fising subsides while supporting small-scale fishers.
